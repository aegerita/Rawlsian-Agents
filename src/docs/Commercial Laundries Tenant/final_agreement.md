2.0 **THE DEMISE**

3.0 **TERM**  
The Tenant will have and hold the Premises, subject to the rents, covenants, conditions, and agreements in this Lease, for a term of **10 years** commencing in **October 1998**, and ending on **[DATE TO BE DETERMINED]** (the “Term”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the terms of this Lease.

Such extensions shall be automatically considered exercised unless notice is provided.

Additionally, the Tenant can extend the initial 10-year term for **an additional 10 years**, contingent upon payment of **the then current market rate for similar laundry room leases**, for any buildings still owned by the Landlord at the end of the initial term.

The Tenant will use the Premises for the purpose of operating laundry facilities and for no other uses without the written consent of the Landlord.

5.0 **RENT**  
5.1 **Annual Rent**  
The Tenant will pay to the Landlord during the Term either **50% of the gross revenue derived from the operation of said equipment**, defined explicitly as all revenue generated before taxes and deductions, or a minimum annual rent of **$[MINIMUM RENT AMOUNT]**, whichever is greater, plus all applicable taxes per annum (the “Rent”). The Tenant shall provide the Landlord with quarterly statements and verifiable documentation of gross revenue to ensure transparency and accuracy in rent calculations.

5.2 **Rent Review**  
The Rent shall be reviewed every two years based on the prevailing economic conditions and market rates for similar properties in the area, ensuring fairness and preventing potential exploitation by either party. Any adjustments to the Rent shall not exceed the greater of a 10% increase from the prior year’s Rent or shall be based on an official market rent appraisal conducted by a mutually agreed-upon independent third party.

5.3 **Major Repairs**  
The costs for any major repairs, defined as any single repair exceeding **[SPECIFY AMOUNT]**, shall be split equally between the Landlord and the Tenant unless otherwise agreed in writing, thus ensuring shared responsibility for significant repairs and maintenance of the Premises.
